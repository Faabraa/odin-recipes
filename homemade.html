Homemade pasta

Homemade pasta is an begginer friendly way to cook aswell as an enjoyable plate for most of the people

Pasta can be made with ingredients that you probably have on your own so you don't even need to buy them

    Ingredients
  • Two cups of flour
  • Fat eggs
  • Two tabblestones of olive oil
  • Teaspoon of salt
  • Water
    Steps
  1. Make the dough
  2. Turn the dough out and kept it for 10 minutes
  3. Let the dough rest
  4. Divide the dough into balls
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes
